Seedlings Schoolhouse & Childcare

Welcome to Seedlings!

Seedlings is a small family childcare home offeering play-bsed, nature inspired program tht supports children's ntural curiosity and desire to learn. I beleive an atmosphere of love and acceptance provides the optimal environment for growth.


I provide a screen-free environment that is intentionally designed to support independence and hands-on, experiential learning. Social-Emotional competence is nurtured through intentional modeling, developmentally appropriate communication skills, and fostering children’s inherent desire to make positive social connections. Our days are filled with time exploring outdoors, reading books, singing songs, and sharing stories, while providing children with individualized care and learning based on their interests and needs. Experiences are curated to stimulate the children’s innate sense of wonder, while providing practical experiences for developing skills for life and later schooling.
